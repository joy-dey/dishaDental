Restorations (Tooth filling)

A dental fillings are used to restore a tooth damaged by decay or damaged part of the tooth which is treated by us using most advanced tooth colored materials and techniques for long term longevity of the tooth.

scalling & root planing (Tooth cleaning)

Our dental clinic provides cleaning of tooth and root planning which is a part of oral hygiene and involves the removal of dental plaque and calculus from the teeth and root surface of the tooth which cannot be cleaned by toothbrush. Keep your teeth healthy and bright with our advanced cleaning technology.

Dental Extraction

Tooth extraction is a process to have a tooth removed usually because of decay, trauma or crowding or due to impaction. All routine and wisdom tooth extractions (removal) are performed with special care by dental surgeon and oral and maxillofacial surgeons in a calm and relaxed environment with full care and comfort of the patient and painlessly as much as possible.

Root Canal Therapy

We specialize in painless Root canal treatment involving removal of infection from inside the tooth & saving natural tooth. A root canal treatment is used to repair and save a tooth that is badly decayed or infected. We are also specialized in Re RCT, By Pass RCT and in all types of Endo dontic treatment of your tooth.

Crown & Bridges

A dental crown or a dental bridge are tooth colored cap which are placed on your RCT treated tooth or in your missing natural tooth to restore its shape, size and function. Disha Dental Care provides treatment like dental crown and dental bridges which is a type of fixed replacement of missing teeth permanently.
